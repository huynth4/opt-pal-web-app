How it works

  1. Take the 4-question quiz. Pick your target industry, the states you'd consider, an optional city, and the company size you're aiming for.
  2. See your matched employers. Your dashboard summarizes total approvals — the count of H-1B petitions that USCIS approved on that employer's behalf for foreign workers across all petition types (new hires, renewals, employer transfers, and amendments). Larger companies that sponsor many international workers every year will have high approval totals; small or occasional sponsors will have lower ones.
  3. Refine and explore. Adjust your filters at any time to compare industries or regions and find the employers that fit your goals.

Why OPT Pal?

Every year USCIS publishes a list of every employer who sponsored H-1B petitions. The raw dataset is huge, the columns are confusing, and pivoting it in a spreadsheet takes time you probably don't have during recruiting season. OPT Pal does the cleanup and lets you slice the data the way an international student or new grad actually thinks about it.

Note: Approval counts reflect historical USCIS data and are not legal advice or a guarantee that an employer will sponsor in the future.
